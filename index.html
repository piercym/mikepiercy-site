The Journey

THE TIMELINE.

1990
Age 15 — The Beginning

Entered the fitness and sports performance world at age 15. From day one, this wasn't just a job — it was an obsession. The foundation of 34+ years of expertise was laid here.

PRO
Professional Athlete

Competed as a professional athlete — living the reality of elite performance from the inside. The lessons learned at this level inform every program, every coaching session, and every keynote delivered today.

2012
Founded The LAB Performance & Sports Science

Built from scratch in Fairfield, NJ. No template, no franchise, no shortcuts. The LAB was designed to be the facility Michael wished had existed when he was an athlete — science-based, athlete-obsessed, results-driven.

2020+
Pandemic, Flood & Rebuild

COVID-19 shuttered The LAB. Then Hurricane Ida flooded it. The industry rallied — colleagues donated time and resources through crowdfunding to help rebuild. The LAB came back stronger. The experience forged an unshakeable operational foundation.

NOW
Training. Speaking. Coaching. Building.

The LAB is thriving. The Playmaker Brief reaches 3,500+ athletes and coaches weekly. Fitness as a Second Language educates coaches globally. International speaking engagements continue. The mission keeps expanding.
